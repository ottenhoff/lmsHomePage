Message Of The Day

  • Learning Management System Experiences Survey

    Brock University is undergoing an evaluation of its current Learning Management System (LMS) and is gathering information about expectations from the Brock University community. All members of the Brock University community are being asked to complete a confidential, quality assurances survey about individuals’ experience with Brock’s current Learning Management System as well as guidance on the importance of functionality and features to inform a future enhanced LMS.

    The survey should take about 10 minutes to complete and is available at: Learning Management System Experiences Survey (Qualtrix)

    Fall 2021 Return to Campus

    Brock is planning to offer as many courses on campus as possible, including labs and other experiential learning, graduate seminars and undergraduate classes for the Fall 2021 academic term. It will mean the much-anticipated return to on-campus learning, teaching and working.

    Read more about the plans for the Fall 2021 academic term.

    Fall/Winter 2021 Timetable Released

    The Fall/Winter 2021 timetable is now live with undergrad registration starting July 6th. In it, students will see a course calendar that reflects a significant return to on-campus instruction with a majority of classes being offered in person, while still having many classes offered in a hybrid online format.

    Explore the timetable at: https://brocku.ca/guides-and-timetables/timetables/.

    Be in the Know! Opportunities for all Brock Students

    There is so much happening at Brock despite Covid-19. The best way to find out what’s being offered by faculties, services, and clubs is to explore Experience BU — Brock’s involvement portal and events calendar https://experiencebu.brocku.ca/.

    Learn more about ExperienceBU and get involved today!
