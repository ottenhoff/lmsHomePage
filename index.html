    Get Paid for Your Lecture Notes

    Student Accessibility Services (SAS) is looking for students to volunteer as notetakers in their classes to help students with disabilities.

    To apply as a note taker, visit the Note Taking registration site at https://brocku.ca/health-wellness-accessibility/sas/

    Students who are hired will receive an honorarium at the end of the semester!
